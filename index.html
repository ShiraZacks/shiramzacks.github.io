About Me

I started out college thinking I would graduate as a social worker. Imagine my surprise when, two years later, the certificate I recieved was for Computer Programming and Web Development!

I then realized that just because I know languages like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SQL, and more, didn't mean I knew how to create a website.

It took a while, but I worked on my skills until I was confident that I could produce an entire website from the bottom up.

So I did! I was hired to create a website for a small business. It was amazing. I was able to use my skills and have fun, doing what I enjoyed and getting paid for it, too! (It hasn't launched yet, click here for a sneak preview!)

Now I am looking foward to moving foward. As I await my next project, I am having fun building projects for myself, and keeping my knowledge up-to-date.
